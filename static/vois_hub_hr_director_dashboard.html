Description (Updated)

Correlated HR storytelling (LLM-based AI commentary) that links HR metrics (L&D adoption, training, Spirit Beat and NPS) to business performance per service tower — to answer 'why' and 'so what', not just present flat data sets side by side.

  • I/O attrition ▲ during platform refresh; cross-check with Passive concentration.

Recommended Actions

  • Tower-level intervention plan for CARE · DE with COO and L&D lead.
  • Unblock VRF approval queue for Project Atlas — target +50 offers / week.
